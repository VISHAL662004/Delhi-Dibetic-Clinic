Our Story

Founded in 1995, MediCare Clinic has been serving the community for over 25 years with unwavering commitment to healthcare excellence. What started as a small family practice has grown into a comprehensive medical facility, always maintaining our core values of compassionate care and medical excellence.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare to every member of our community. Today, we continue to honor that commitment with state-of-the-art facilities, cutting-edge medical technology, and a team of dedicated healthcare professionals.
